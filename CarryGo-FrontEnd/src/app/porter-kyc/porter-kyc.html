Terms & Conditions

By registering as a CarryGo commuter porter, you agree to:

  • Deliver parcels safely, securely, and on time without tampering
  • Maintain a minimum customer rating of 4.0 / 5.0
  • Comply with all applicable traffic laws and road safety regulations
  • Accept CarryGo's commission structure of 15% per completed delivery
  • Notify the platform immediately if a parcel is lost, damaged, or delayed

Privacy Policy

CarryGo collects your GPS location data during active deliveries solely to enable live parcel tracking for senders. Your personal data is encrypted at rest and in transit and is never sold to third parties. You may request data deletion at any time by contacting support@carrygo.in.

Commission & Payout Policy

Earnings are credited to your registered bank account every Monday for the previous week's completed deliveries. A minimum payout threshold of ₹200 applies. CarryGo reserves the right to withhold payment pending resolution of any active dispute.
